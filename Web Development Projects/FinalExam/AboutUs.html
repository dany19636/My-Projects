About Us

The Chicago Park District has been an integral part of the community for over a century, dedicated to preserving and enriching the quality of life in Chicago. With over 600 parks spanning the city, our mission is to provide world-class recreational opportunities, foster community engagement, and promote a healthy lifestyle.

Our facilities include sports fields, playgrounds, swimming pools, and cultural spaces, ensuring there is something for everyone. We take pride in hosting a variety of programs and events that bring people together and celebrate the vibrant diversity of our city.

Our Mission

"To enhance the quality of life in Chicago by providing accessible and diverse recreational opportunities, cultural programs, and green spaces for everyone."
